My Process

How I work

Strategy without execution is a slide deck. Execution without strategy is noise. I do both.

01 — Discovery

Understand the business

Before I touch a caption, I dig into your goals, audience, competitors, and what success actually means for your business — not your Instagram.

02 — Strategy

Build the system

Platform-specific strategy with clear content pillars, format mix, cadence, and KPIs tied to revenue — not vanity metrics.

03 — Execution

Create and distribute

I handle content creation, scheduling, community management, and creative direction — consistently, at quality, without dropping balls.

04 — Optimize

Analyze and improve

Regular performance reviews with data-backed decisions. What's working gets scaled. What isn't gets cut or rebuilt from a better angle.
